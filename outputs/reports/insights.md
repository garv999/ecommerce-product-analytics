# Executive Summary

This project analyzed customer behavior and revenue patterns for an e-commerce dataset using **Python and SQL**.

## Key Findings

- Revenue is concentrated among a small group of high-value customers.
- Customer retention declines significantly after the first purchase.
- RFM segmentation identified key customer groups such as **Champions, Loyal Customers, and At-Risk users**.
- Customer Lifetime Value analysis shows that increasing retention and purchase frequency can significantly improve revenue.

## Strategic Recommendations

1. Focus marketing efforts on high-value customer segments.
2. Improve early retention through targeted promotions after the first purchase.
3. Implement loyalty programs for frequent buyers.
4. Use customer segmentation to personalize marketing campaigns.
